About

Hi everyone! My name is Eugene Kim, and I'm a project manager, entrepreneur, musician, and developer. Born and raised in Chicago and the Greater Chicago Area, I'm an Economics graduate from Northwestern University.

Among my wide array of hard and soft skills, programming is one of my creative outlets. As such, I enjoy tinkering with a few projects here and there, many of which are listed out below.

Otherwise, in my free time, I enjoy producing music, playing video games, practicing Kumdo, and bowling. Some of my proudest moments include: In 2019, I won the OnRecord songwriting competition with the song 'she,' a collaboration with Will Ouyang and Ross Patten, which you can check out on YouTube here! In 2020, I had the opportunity to talk about some of my experiences as a Korean American and passions with Voice of America, which you can check out on YouTube here!
